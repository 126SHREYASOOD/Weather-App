About This App

This weather app allows you to check real-time weather updates for any city in the world. Enter your city name and get temperature, humidity, and weather conditions instantly.

Now, you can also view the weather forecast for the next 7 days.

Features

  • Real-time temperature, humidity, and weather conditions.
  • 7-day weather forecast with detailed daily insights.
  • Interactive weather icons representing the current weather.
  • Supports searching for any city worldwide.

Weather Safety Tips

Stay prepared with these weather safety tips:

  • Check the forecast before planning outdoor activities.
  • Dress accordingly to the weather conditions.
  • Stay hydrated and protect yourself from extreme temperatures.
  • Keep an emergency kit ready for severe weather conditions.
